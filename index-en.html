ClassicalComposers: Description back to ToC

The Musical Composition Ontology is designed to represent entities related to the field of music, specifically focusing on composers, compositions, countries associated with composers, musical genres, and historical eras. The ontology aims to provide a structured framework for describing and categorizing elements within the domain of classical music including Jazz and Blues
